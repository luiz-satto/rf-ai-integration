General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance
Introduction

At Artima.ai, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This GDPR compliance page outlines the practices and policies we have implemented to ensure that we are in full compliance with the GDPR regulations.

Personal Data We Collect

1. User-Provided Information: This includes but is not limited to, names, email addresses, and other personal information provided during account registration or through the use of our services.

2. Automatically Collected Information:We collect data regarding user interactions with our platform, such as login information, user preferences, and usage statistics.

3. Data from Integrated Social Media Platforms: We gather information from connected social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn) as permitted by users and in accordance with the privacy policies of those platforms.

1. Service Provision:Personal data is used to provide, maintain, and improve our AI social management services.

2. Communication: We use personal information to communicate with users regarding service updates, technical notices, and customer support.

3. Analytics and Improvement: Data is analyzed to enhance our platform's functionality and user experience.

4. Legal Compliance: Where necessary, data may be processed for legal compliance purposes.

Data Sharing and Transfer

1. Service Providers:We may share data with third-party service providers for functions such as hosting, analytics, and customer service.

2. Legal Requirements:We may disclose personal data if required by law or in response to legal processes.

3. Consent-Based Sharing:With user consent, we may share information with third parties for broader service functionality.

Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR, users have the right to:

1. Access personal data held by Artima.ai.

2. Request correction or deletion of their personal data.

3. Object to or restrict processing of their personal data.

4. Request data portability.

5. Withdraw consent at any time, if processing is based on consent.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy, in compliance with our legal obligations.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy
